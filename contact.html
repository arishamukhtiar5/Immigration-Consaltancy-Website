FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions our clients ask before getting started.

How long does the visa process take?
Processing times vary by visa type and destination country. Generally: Visit Visas take 2–4 weeks, Study Visas 4–8 weeks, Work Visas 4–12 weeks, and PR applications 6–18 months. We always provide estimated timelines during the free consultation.
What is the consultation fee?
Your first consultation is completely FREE. During this 30-minute session, we'll assess your case, recommend the right visa pathway, and provide you with a transparent fee structure for our services. There are no hidden charges.
What if my visa gets rejected?
While we maintain a 98% success rate, in rare rejection cases we analyze the reasons, address the issues, and prepare a stronger re-application. We stand by our clients until they achieve their goals. We also offer a detailed review service for previously rejected cases.
Can I apply for a visa without IELTS?
It depends on your destination country and visa type. Many countries now accept alternatives to IELTS (like PTE, TOEFL, Duolingo). Some visas (e.g., certain work and visit visas) don't require English tests at all. We assess your specific case during the free consultation.
Do you provide online consultancy services?
Yes! We provide complete online consultation services via WhatsApp, Zoom, and phone call. Clients from across Pakistan and internationally can avail our full range of services remotely. All documentation can also be submitted and processed digitally.
